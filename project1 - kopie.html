Adore: An Opening To Contemplation

Expertise areas

  • Creativity & Aesthetics
  • Technology & Realisation
  • Math, Computing & Data

Final Master Project

Feb - Jun 2024

ADORE is an explorative Research-through-Design (RtD) project centered on Interactivity, Morality, and Embodiment. It aims to invite individuals to reflect on their relationship with technology and moral values through an interaction with ADORE, which is a door that opens and closes with worship-like gestures. Inspired by cultural critiques and ethical theories, ADORE challenges the notion that moral judgments are purely rational, emphasizing embodied experiences and moral emotions. The iterative design process, involving making several prototypes, observations and interviews, revealed themes such as the perception of agency, moral tensions from anthropomorphized technology, designer intentionality, and the role of ambiguity in supporting contemplation. ADORE contributes to the design field by promoting a vision-based approach and offering an interactive installation for public engagement, highlighting the potential of design for deeper moral and philosophical exploration.

Keywords: ADORE, Interactional Morality, Moral Foundation Theory, Embodiment, Aesthetics of Intelligence, Vision-Based Design, Experiential Speculation, Research through Design
